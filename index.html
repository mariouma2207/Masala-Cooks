Ingredients:
  • 500 grams couscous (semolina couscous)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 zucchinis, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas (washed and soaked)
  • 500 grams meat (lamb or chicken), cut into medium-sized pieces
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup water (or meat/chicken broth)
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional)
Instructions: 1. Prapare the Couscous
  • In a bowl, place the couscous and add a pinch of salt and olive oil.
  • Gradually add water and mix the couscous with your hands until it absorbs the water.
  • Steam the couscous in a couscousier (or a steamer) for about 15-20 minutes, then return it to the bowl and drizzle with a little more oil.
2. Prepare the sauce:
  • In a large pot, heat the olive oil and add the chopped onion and garlic. Saut√© until softened.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for about 5 minutes.
  • Add the meat (chicken or lamb) and stir until it changes color.
  • Add the carrots, zucchinis, and chickpeas.
  • Add the spices (turmeric, chili powder, cumin, coriander, salt, and black pepper) and mix well.
  • Pour in the water or broth and bring to a boil. Then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes until the meat and vegetables are cooked through.
